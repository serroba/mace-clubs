Mace & Clubs

A metronome-driven workout app for steel mace and Indian club training. Swing on tempo, follow interval sets, and log it all to Garmin Connect — built for the Garmin Instinct 3.

Features

● Tempo you can feel

A drift-free metronome from 20 to 240 bpm with wrist vibration and tone on every beat — you keep your 360s smooth without watching the screen.

● Interval presets

Pick a workout shape before you start: 5×2:00 with 1:00 or 2:00 rest, 3×2:00, EMOM-style 10×1:00, or free training. The watch calls work and rest for you.

● Real training data

Sessions record as a named “Mace & Clubs” strength activity in Garmin Connect with heart rate, training load, and a per-session sets field in the FIT file.

● Set up from your phone

Default tempo, beep, and vibration are app settings in Garmin Connect — adjust them between sessions without touching watch menus.

Build & install

The app targets the Instinct 3 Solar (45 mm / 50 mm) and isn’t on the Connect IQ Store yet — for now you build from source with the Connect IQ SDK:

git clone https://github.com/serroba/mace-clubs.git
cd mace-clubs

# build (developer key: see the README)
monkeyc -f monkey.jungle -d instinct3solar45mm \
        -o bin/mace-clubs.prg -y /path/to/developer_key

# run in the simulator ...
connectiq && monkeydo bin/mace-clubs.prg instinct3solar45mm

# ... or sideload: copy bin/mace-clubs.prg to GARMIN/APPS over USB
