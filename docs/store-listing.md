## App name
Mace and Clubs

## Summary (one line)
Metronome and interval timer for steel mace and Indian club training.

## Description
Keep your swing cadence without watching the screen. Mace and Clubs is a
metronome and interval timer built for steel mace and Indian club training.

- Drift-free metronome, 5–240 bpm, with wrist vibration and an optional
  beep. An accented downbeat marks the start of each loop, so you feel a
  side switch instead of reading it.
- Loop patterns per workout: a steady cadence, or the classic club 4-2.
- Workout presets: timed intervals with rest, EMOM-style, free training,
  or a custom shape you set from your phone.
- A five-second start countdown gives you time to get into position, and a
  five-second vibration/tone warning announces the next work interval.
- On-screen rounds, heart rate, and set count. Sessions save to Garmin
  Connect as a strength activity with heart rate and a per-session set count.
- Change settings on the watch — hold MENU for beep/vibration, cue
  frequency, and whether the corner shows rounds or heart rate.
- Optional motion capture logs accelerometer features each second for
  offline swing analysis.
